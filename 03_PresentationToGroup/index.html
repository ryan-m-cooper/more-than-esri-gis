Leadership Scott County 2016-2017: The Case for Scroggin Park

All Leadership Scott County classes are tasked with completing a project in the community. The 2016-2017 class is no different. We the Project Research Committee have done some research to help narrow down a site for ideas for our project. Here we outline our research, analysis, and recommendation.

Past LSC Projects

Past Leadership Scott County have done projects throughout Georgetown. We wanted to see if we could do a project in an area where there hasn't been much work done by Leadership Scott County. To figure this out, we found data that showed the locations of previous Leadership Scott County projects. We generated buffers of various distances around the project locations to try and determine if there were areas underserved by previous LSC projects.

Buffers of 1/10 mi and 1/4 mi still left a lot of Georgetown to sift through. The 1.0 mi buffer left very little. Examining Georgetown outside of the 1/2 mi buffers presented some intriguing options.

Scroggin Park

The 1/2 mile buffer around each project limited the possible areas we could look for a project in. Examining what was left, some areas are well-off neighborhoods while others are industrial or commercial areas. However, one spot stuck out to us: Scroggin Park. Scroggin Park is run by the Georgetown Housing Authority and provides public housing for low-income residents.

Field Inspection: Basketball Court

James went out into the field to gather some information about Scroggin Park and to see if there might be some project possibilities. One element that stood out was the basketball court.

Others with more knowledge of the park have said the basketball court is a popular place for local kids to play basketball. The court is in decent shape, but there is grass growing in some cracks in the pavement. The lines are also a bit faded. The goals have new nets, but one of them is pulling away from the ground!

Field Inspection: Open Field

South of the basketball fields is an open field. It is relatively flat and looks to be another great area for children to play and community members to gather.

Field Inspection: Shelter

There is a really nice shelter with picnic tables. A great place for people to gather and create community!

Recommendation: Basketball Court Maintenance at Scroggin Park

Scroggin Park has a lot of good things going for it. It is centrally located within the Scorggin Park Neighborhood, has a nice field, and there's a great shelter with picnic tables. While some work has been done to maintain the basketball court, it doesn't quite match up to the quality of other parts of the park. We recommend that our group focus our efforts on sprucing up the basketball court in Scroggin Park in order to make it a safe, fun community facility.
